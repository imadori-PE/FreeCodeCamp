My Ceviche Recipe

Here’s a classic Peruvian ceviche (cebiche) recipe — fresh, citrusy, and full of flavor

Ingredients

  • 500 g (1 lb) fresh firm white fish (e.g., caballa, sea bass, or tilapia), cut into 1–2 cm cubes
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1–2 ají limo or chili peppers, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • Juice of 8–10 lemons (enough to cover the fish)
  • Salt (about 1 tsp or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • A few cilantro (coriander) leaves, chopped

Instructions

  1. Cut the fish into even cubes and keep it chilled.
  2. Place it in a non-metallic bowl
  3. Add salt and mix gently with the fish for about 30 seconds — this starts the curing process.
  4. Add chopped chili, garlic (if using), and cilantro.
  5. Squeeze fresh lemons and pour the juice over the fish until it’s just covered.
  6. Mix everything lightly.
  7. Add more salt or chili if needed.
  8. Serve with lettuce, sweet potato, and corn on the side.
